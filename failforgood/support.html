FailForGood lets you create goal-based challenges with friends, submit proof of completion, judge submissions, and cheer each other on. If you need help, have a question, or want to report a problem, please get in touch.

Common questions

  • How do I report or block someone? In the App, tap the report or block option on a user's profile, a chat message, or a proof submission. Reported content is reviewed and may be hidden or removed.
  • How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Delete Account in the App. This permanently anonymises your account and removes your uploaded content.
